Important input validation

[a-z] = all lowercase letters [0-9] = all digits [A-Z] = all uppercase letters [a-zA-z] = combination of lower and upper case letters "\d"+ = 0-9 all numbers. + means at least one or more number [a-z0-9] = lowercase letters and all numbers combination [a-z]{10} = you can use all lowercase followed by 10 chara 01[0-9]{2,11} = must put 0 and 1 and condition allows all numbers with more than 2 numbers and less than 11 (?=.*[A-Za-z])= mean that user must enter at least one upper or lower letter (?=.*\d) = mean that user must enter at least one digit [A-Za-z\d]{8,} = allows upper & lower letters with a minimum 8 characters.
